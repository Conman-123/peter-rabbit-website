Peter

Peter Rabbit is one of the four Rabbit children. He is the naughtiest of them all, and often finds himself in all sorts of trouble. In the book, Peter ventures into Mr McGregor's garden to eat his precious vegetables, but he comes dangerously close to being caught. He manages to escape, but at the cost of his shoes and his little blue jacket.

Flopsy

Flopsy is one of the four Rabbit children. She is a good little bunny, behaving herself and never doing anything naughty. In the book, while Peter is exploring Mr McGregor's garden, Flopsy and her sisters are picking blackberries for their supper.

Mrs Rabbit

Mrs Rabbit is the four Rabbit children's mother. She loves them all very much, but she gets angry when Peter doesn't listen to her. However, even when Peter returns without his precious clothes, Mrs Rabbit still makes him some camomile tea in bed.

Mr McGregor

Mr McGregor is an old man who lives near the Rabbit family. He tends to his precious garden every day, and is always on the lookout for naughty rabbits who are trying to eat his vegetables. In the book he nearly catches Peter Rabbit, who just manages to escape at the cost of his shoes and jacket.

Mrs McGregor

Mrs McGregor is Mr McGregor's wife. She hates the rabbits as much as her husband; she even put Mr Rabbit in a pie!
